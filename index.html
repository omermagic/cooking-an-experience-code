Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Where is it?

The experience takes place in Valbom, in the Porto area. Most guests arrive by Uber or Bolt. The exact address is shared after booking.

Do I need baking experience?

No. The experience is designed to be friendly and accessible, even if you have never made éclairs before.

Is it a full dinner?

No. It includes wine, cheese, crackers, and dessert tasting. It works beautifully before or after a light meal.

Can we book a private group?

Yes. Private groups of up to 6 guests can request a custom date, depending on availability.
